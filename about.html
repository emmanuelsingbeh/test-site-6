Word From Our CEO

At Liftinghands NGO, we believe that true change begins with compassion and is sustained through action. Every child who walks into our school, every family we reach through our health, agricultural, and empowerment programs represents a story of resilience, hope, and potential waiting to be unlocked.

Our vision is simple but powerful: to lift lives through education, opportunity, and community. We understand that poverty is not just the absence of resources, but often the absence of access, support, and belief. That’s why we commit ourselves daily to building bridges between need and opportunity, between communities and solutions, and between present challenges and future successes. Through the dedicated work of our staff, the support of our partners, and the incredible strength of the communities we serve, we are witnessing transformation—not just in statistics, but in smiles, stories, and futures. Education is more than a classroom experience; it is the seed of generational change.

As CEO, I am proud to lead an organization that not only uplifts but listens. That not only provides aid but builds capacity. And that never loses sight of the human heart behind every initiative. Together, with your support, we will continue to rise—lifting hands, changing lives, and shaping a brighter tomorrow.

Our Mission

At Liftinghands NGO, we are dedicated to fostering sustainable development and empowering communities through education, job creation, agriculture, health initiatives, and charitable actions. Our mission is to uplift individuals and families by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to thrive.

Our Commitment

We believe in the transformative power of education and its ability to break the cycle of poverty, promote health and wellbeing, and enhance livelihoods. We commit ourselves to creating a supportive environment where collaboration, innovation, and compassion are at the forefront of our efforts.

Our Vision

At Liftinghands NGO, we envision a world where every individual is empowered through education, equipped for meaningful employment, and supported by a compassionate community. Our commitment is to break the cycle of poverty, fostering a society in which everyone has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to the greater good.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do and shape our approach to community service

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and understanding, recognizing the dignity and worth of every individual.

Community

We believe in the power of community and work collaboratively to create sustainable positive change.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our programs, maintaining high standards in education and community service.

Impact

We focus on creating measurable, lasting impact that transforms lives and strengthens communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Lifting Hands NGO began as a small community initiative with a big dream: to provide quality education and essential services to underserved communities.

What started as a grassroots effort has grown into a comprehensive organization that operates Jomuel High Star Elementary School and runs multiple community development programs across various regions.

Together, we strive to lift lives, inspire aspirations, and create lasting change that resonates across generations. By engaging with local communities and stakeholders, we strive to build sustainable solutions that respect cultural diversity and promote social equity.
